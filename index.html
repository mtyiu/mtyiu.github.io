Biography

Matt YIU, Man Tung received the M.Phil. degree under the supervision of Prof. Patrick P. C. Lee in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the Chinese University of Hong Kong. He received his B.Sc degree in Computer Science (first-class honors) from the same department, with minors in Information Engineering (Computer Networking stream) and Mathematics in 2014.

His research interests include in-memory distributed storage systems and the applications of erasure coding in these systems.
